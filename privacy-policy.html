Privacy Policy for World of Stories Alexa Skill

Last Updated: August 15, 2023

This privacy policy describes how Anupam Jain ("I", "me", or "my") handles your information when you use the World of Stories Alexa skill.

Information Collection

The World of Stories skill does not collect or store any personal information from users. When you interact with the skill:

  • No personal data is collected or stored
  • No voice recordings are saved
  • No user account information is accessed
  • No location data is requested or stored

Session Data

The skill uses temporary session data to remember which story was last told to you during a single session, to enable the "repeat story" feature. This data is temporary and is automatically deleted when you end your session with the skill.

Third-Party Services

This skill does not use any third-party analytics services or advertising platforms. The skill runs on Amazon Web Services and is subject to the Amazon Web Services Privacy Notice.

Children's Privacy

This skill is suitable for users of all ages, including children. As mentioned above, we do not collect any personal information from any users, including children under 13.

Changes to this Policy

If I decide to change this privacy policy, I will post those changes on this page and update the "Last Updated" date above.
